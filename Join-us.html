Head - Growth Marketing

Position Overview:

We are seeking a highly experienced hands-on Full-Stack Growth Marketing Head to accelerate acquisition and activation of our user base using all relevant marketing channels. The ideal candidate will have between 8-10 years of experience in growth marketing and will be responsible for developing and executing comprehensive marketing strategies to take Apurva to the Global South (e.g., India, Africa, South America etc). This is a B2B marketing role focused on Not-for-profits, Foundations, Governments, Research & Think Tanks etc.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and Execute Marketing Strategies for acquisition and activation
  • Create and manage marketing campaigns across social media platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube), email newsletters, and other digital channels

Customer Acquisition and Retention

  • Develop and execute targeted global marketing campaigns to acquire new organisations
  • Design and implement customer retention strategies to ensure high customer satisfaction and loyalty

Brand Positioning and Messaging

  • Define and refine Apurva.ai's brand positioning and messaging to resonate with our target audience
  • Ensure consistent branding across all marketing materials and channels

Marketing Automation and Analytics

  • Implement and manage marketing automation tools to streamline marketing processes and improve efficiency
  • Analyse and report on marketing performance using data analytics tools to optimise marketing strategies

Content Creation and Management

  • Develop and manage a content calendar to ensure consistent and high-quality content creation
  • Create and curate content (blog posts, videos, social media posts) that resonates with our target audience

Event Management

  • Plan and execute marketing events (webinars, workshops, conferences) to build brand awareness

Experience:

  • 8-10 years of experience in SaaS growth marketing, with a focus on customer acquisition and retention
  • Proficiency in marketing automation, analytics, and content creation tools
  • Deep understanding of SaaS business models, customer personas and target markets
  • Proven track record of driving successful marketing and communication strategies achieving targets in a SaaS environment
  • Experience with content creation and management
  • Familiarity with event management and planning
  • Entrepreneurial, self-starter, and passionate about building and growing startups
  • Ability to analyse marketing metrics and data-driven mindset to translate to actions
  • Experience working in the social sector would be a plus

Application Requirements:

  • Resume
  • Examples of your Work
  • A short video (1-2Mins) about your experience & evidence of being a successful Marketing and Communication leader, plus, what you plan to do as the Head of Growth Marketing of Apurva.ai based on your current understanding of the organisation and the role Send these to contact@apurva.ai.

About Apurva.ai:

Apurva.ai is a not-for-profit organisation housed in the Centre for Exponential Change, headquartered in Bangalore, India. We leverage exponential technologies to help social change leaders solve large, complex societal problems with the power of collective wisdom and create impact at scale. Our mission is to empower social change leaders to unlock collective wisdom within their networks for effective and sustainable problem-solving.
