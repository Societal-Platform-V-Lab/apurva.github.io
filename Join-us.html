Communications Lead (Social Media and External Communications)

Apurva.ai is a not-for-profit organisation housed in the Centre for Exponential Change, headquartered in Bangalore, India. Apurva.ai is a unique platform leveraging exponential technologies to help social change leaders solve large, complex societal problems with the power of collective wisdom and create impact at scale. Deeply rooted in Trust, Inclusion and Emergence, it helps amplify the voice of communities, the power of conversations, and institutional knowledge across various domains and regions through multiple languages and channels.

As a Communications Lead spearheading the Social Media and External communications efforts to share the narrative and impact of Apurva.ai to a global audience. This role will be responsible for defining Apurva’s brand positioning, marketing and communications strategies to help increase brand awareness, engage users on digital platforms like Twitter, LinkedIn, and Emails and manage the messaging for our marquee events (own and ones we will participate in). 

Responsibilities:

Working across various communication channels (Social Media Channels like Linkedin, Twitter, Youtube / Linkedin groups / Broadcast Emails and Newsletters and others) to create distinct strategies for different channels.

Social Media

  • Develop and execute a comprehensive social media strategy aligned with the organisation's goals and values.
  • Create high-quality, engaging, visually appealing content for various social media platforms, including text, images, videos, and infographics.
  • Monitor all social media channels, respond to comments, messages and mentions promptly, engage with relevant posts and foster meaningful interactions with the online community.
  • Identify and engage with influencers and partners to expand our reach.
  • Use social media analytics tools to track key performance metrics, such as engagement rate, reach, conversions, and ROI.
  • Continuously optimise strategy based on data and insights.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices to continually refine and optimise the strategy. Plan, execute and manage social media campaigns and events
  • Collaborate with other teams based on the requirement and implement the action items.

Website 

  • Update website content as and when required
  • Perform cross-traffic analysis of Website/Blogs Published/Social Media

External Communications

  • Manage inbound and outbound communications across various channels like emails, social media, etc.
  • Create and curate content from various sources for reports, newsletters/emailers, case studies, podcasts and other audio/video communication assets
  • Define PR/external media communications strategy, build the pipeline for publishing articles and work with external agencies to execute it

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, digital media, or a related field with at least 4-5 years of experience
  • Proven work experience in social media management and strategy development
  • Experience in PR would be an added advantage
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills with an understanding of brand voice and tone
  • Excellent editing, and proofreading skills
  • A storyteller with creative thinking ability to generate engaging content
  • Excellent organisational skills and the ability to manage multiple tasks and deadlines
  • Strong problem-solving skills and a proactive attitude.
  • In-depth knowledge of social media platforms, trends, and best practices relevant to the work we do
  • Proficiency in social media management tools and analytics platforms
  • Data-driven mindset with the ability to analyse and interpret social media and website metrics
  • Interest and understanding in any of our focus domains (Education, Health, Livelihood, Equity and Climate) would be an added advantage
  • Experience working in the social sector would be a plus

Job Type: Full-time

Location: This Position is based out of Bangalore
