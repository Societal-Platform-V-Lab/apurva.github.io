Marketing / Outreach Lead

We are looking for a marketing/outreach lead for Apurva.ai, a specialised platform designed to support efforts in the development sector. With rapidly increasing complex social problems, solutions need to evolve and keep pace. Apurva.ai aims to provide change leaders with the collective wisdom of their network to more effectively and rapidly address these challenges. Housed in the EkStep Foundation, Apurva.ai is uniquely positioned to make a significant impact in the development sector.

As the marketing/outreach lead, you will be responsible for building awareness about the platform within the development sector. Your main focus will be on the following areas:

  • Website: Ensuring that the website is engaging and informative for visitors. Creating various types of content, including blog posts, demos, videos, case studies, references and infographics that showcase Apurva's value. The focus should be to produce a steady stream of high-quality content that encourages social sharing, engagement and keeps visitors coming back for more.

  • Social Media: Develop and implement social media strategies that increase engagement and awareness of the product. Curate and create high-quality content that resonates with the target audience, and drives interaction and engagement.

  • Events: Plan and facilitate physical and virtual events that showcase Apurva.

  • Targeted Outreach: Reach out to new and existing networks and foster awareness and engagement.

Requirements:

  • The ideal candidate should have a minimum of 5-8 years of experience leading impactful marketing and outreach programmes in the development sector.

  • We are open to considering volunteers who are passionate about using their marketing skills to solve complex problems, as well as consultants who have extensive experience in leading outreach and marketing efforts.

  • This is a part-time position.

  • The position is based out of Bangalore.
