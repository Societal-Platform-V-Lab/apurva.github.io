Our mission

Our mission is to empower Change Leaders to leverage the power of Apurva and create impact at scale.


Solving large and complex problems requires a deep understanding of the overall system and its interdependencies.
Change Leaders tackling such societal problems are unable to harness the collective wisdom of their network, limiting their ability to solve with speed and scale.
Apurva helps harness this collective wisdom.

Meet the founding team

Nandan Nilekani

Advisor

Nandan co-founded Infosys and has been its non-executive chairman since 2017. He is the co-founder and chairman of EkStep Foundation, a not-for-profit effort to create a learner-centric platform to improve literacy and numeracy for millions of children. Nandan is the founding chairman of the unique identification of India. Times magazine listed him as one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2006 and 2009. Foreign Policy magazine listed him as one of the top 100 global thinkers in 2010. Nandan is the author of Imagining India and co-author of Rebooting India: Realizing a Billion Aspirations.

Sanjay Purohit

Mentor

Sanjay has more than 30 years of diverse and global leadership experience across corporate and societal development sectors. Since 2016, at EkStep Foundation, he is leading the evolution of Societal Platform Thinking, an innovative method to resolve complex societal challenges with speed, at scale, and sustainably. He advises multiple social entrepreneurs who are developing Societal Platforms. He catalyzed ShikshaLokam, a Societal Platform focused on developing leadership across the education system in India. He advises startups supported by Fundamentum, a scale investment fund, The/Nudge CSI, a not-for-profit incubator and accelerator, and Edumentum, a not-for-profit incubator focused on education. He is the Chair of - Innovation Advisor Board at Unleash, and an Advisory Board Member at Sparks & Honey.

Anand Rajan

Mission Leader

Anand is an experienced senior practitioner with 25 years of diverse global expertise, spanning both the corporate and development sectors. He currently serves as the Chief Advisor for Societal Thinking at EkStep Foundation, where he enables organizations to apply platform thinking to solve complex social problems. Anand also guides change leaders in their journey of scale transformation. Anand brings a blend of technology, solution and scale thinking required to solve large, complex and dynamic problems. Anand runs the V-Lab at Societal Thinking, a prototyping lab for change leaders to create digital prototypes for their solutions. His work cuts across various domains including Education, Livelihood, Health, and Equity and Climate.
